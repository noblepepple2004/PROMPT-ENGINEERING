# Engineering Study Prompt

## Objective

Create concise study notes for engineering students preparing for examinations.

## Prompt

Explain the process of gas dehydration.

Your answer should include:

- Definition
- Purpose
- Common methods
- Advantages
- Disadvantages
- Industrial applications
- Examination tips

The explanation should be clear enough for a university engineering student.

---

## Why This Prompt Works

This prompt provides clear instructions, defines the audience, and specifies the exact structure expected in the response. It helps produce organized and consistent study material.
